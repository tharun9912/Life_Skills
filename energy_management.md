**4. Sleep is superpower**

- Sleep is essential for physical and mental well being.

- Lack of sufficient sleep harms memory and learning ability.

- Sleep increases our immune health.
  
- Sleep before and after learning strenghtens memory capacity.
  
- Having regular sleep times and a cool bedroom increases sleeps quality. 
  
**5 My ideas to sleep better :** 

- Avoid usage of mobile phone in the middle of sleep.
  
- Try to sleep in a calm environment.
  
- Keep the phone on silent mode. 
  
- Follow regular sleep timings.
