# Focus Management

## 1. Deep Work

- Working with full focus on a single task without any distractions.
  
- Giving one full attention to one important task for a period of time.
  
- It is a high quality work.
  
## 2. How to do deep work ?

- Schedule your distractions periods at home and work.
  
- Develop consistent deep work habit .
  
- Transform deep work into a simple regular habit.
  
- Follow evening shutdown method , record all the completed tasks on that day and tasks needed to complete for the following day.
  
- Evening shutdown method helps to disconnect from the rest of the day .
  
## 3. Implementation of deep work principles in my life:

- Schedule one hour in the morning for deep work.
  
- Avoid using mobile phones during office work.
  
## 4. Dangers of social media 

- Mental health issues.
  
- Sleep disruption
  
- Reduce ability to concentrate for a long time.
  
- Reduces your productivity.
  
- Constant exposure to social media can make you anxious and depressed.
