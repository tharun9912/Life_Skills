# Grit and Growth

1. **Summary**
- A person's success does not depend only  on IQ or talent,it also requires the passion to work hard continuously.
  
2. **Summary**
- Person's with fixed mindset believe skills are built, they can't change.
- But person's with growth mindset believe skills can be learn and can improve knowledge and learn things easily.
- Growth mindset is the foundation for learning.

3. **Internal Locus of control**
- It is defined as the degree tpo which you believe you have control over your life.
- According to this video, if you want to stay motivated, keep believe in you, and try to focus on your internal things which you have control.
  
4. **Growth Mindset can be learned by :**
-  Always believe in your abilities.
-  Question your assumptions that limit your work and success.
-  Develop you own set of life curriculum.
-  Try to develop healthy mindset.
  
5. **My action to built growth mindset :**
- Believe in your abilities.
- Don't worry about external things, on which you do not have control.
- Stop fearing about results.
- Work hard.
