# Learning Process

## 1. Feynman Technique

- Explaining the concept  you learned using simple language.


## 2. Pomodoro Technique

-  Pomodoro technique is interesting idea for me.
-  Work with full focus for about 25 minutes.
-  Take a small break.
-  This process improves your learning capacity.

## 3. Active and Diffused modes of thinking 

- Active mode activates when you are fully concentrated on the task.
- Diffuse mode of thinking is relaxed mode. 
- Diffuse mode works in the background.
- Diffuse mode don't need much concentration..

## 4. Steps to take when approaching a new topic according to the given video 

- Learn basic things first, to get an idea.
- Eliminate distractions and obstacles that prevents you from practising   the topic.
- Practice the important things in topic for at least 20 hours.


## 5. I'm going to develop below habits to improve learning process.

- I keep my phone on silent.
- Avoid listening to music or watching videos while practicing.
- solve more problems related to topic.
- Sleeping for at least 8 hours to keep brain active and healthy.
