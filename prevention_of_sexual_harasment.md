# Prevention Of Sexual Harassment

1. **Behaviour that cause sexual harassment :-**
      - Making sexual comments, jokes and double-meaning remarks.
  
      - Staring, making gestures, or showing and offensive content.
  
      - Sending inappropriate messages,images or videos.
  
      - Threatening someone’s job, promotion, or evaluation in exchange for sexual favours
  
      - Physical contacts like hugging and touching person without their consent.


2. **Steps to take during such behaviour:-**

   - First tell the person to stop .  

   -  Report the matter to your company’s Internal Committee or related staff in company.
  
   - Support the affected person if you are a witness and try to convince the person to not involve in these type of activities.
  
   - Record the incident through mobile or other devices for future references.

   - Try to stay away from people who create problems, if possible.
