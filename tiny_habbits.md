# Tiny Habits

## 1. Interesting idea
- Getting success or improvement through tiny habits.
- Instead of giving much attention to motivation, it focused on habits which are easy to perform.
- Motivation won't stay for long time , but changing tiny habits can stay.

## 2. MAP
- **M** stands for motivation,**A** stands for ability , **P** stands for Prompt.  
- I will use motivation to work on the thing for a long time.
- Use ability to  make work easier and faster.
- Use prompt to remind me my goals and targets.
  
## 3. Importance of celebrate
- Celebration gives you a energy, that energy motivates you to work hard.
- It keeps you motivated, which is needed to give constant efforts.
- And it makes you calm and healthy.
  
## 4. Interesting idea
- The idea i found most interesting is that making tiny improvements ,just 1% better each day can lead to big changes over time.

## 5. About Identity
- According to book, you develop habits based on what you think about yourself, not just what you want to achieve.
- If you act like the person you want to become, your identity changes over time.
  
## 6. About habits
- If you want to make habits easier, try to start small, just do tiny version of it so it feels easy and you will develop over the time.
  
## 7.Making habit harder
- To make habit harder, change the environment,so you will forget slowly.
